## Target Audience

### Primary: Residential Homeowners & Renters
- Families moving between homes in the 30A / Destin / Fort Walton area
- Retirees relocating to the Panhandle (high-value, trust-sensitive)
- Vacation rental owners moving furnishings between properties
- Military families (Eglin AFB, Hurlburt Field) — frequent movers, value reliability

### Secondary: Small Business Owners
- Local restaurants, boutiques, offices relocating along the Emerald Coast
- Short-term rental (Airbnb/VRBO) operators needing delivery & staging help

### Psychographic Profile
- Values local over corporate
- Willing to pay more for trustworthiness
- Makes decisions based on reviews and referrals
- Anxious about moving — wants reassurance upfront

## Voice & Tone

### The Brand Voice Is:
- **Warm** — friendly, approachable, like a trusted neighbor
- **Confident** — we know what we're doing; no hedging
- **Professional** — this is a licensed business, not a couple of guys with a truck
- **Local** — mentions of the area, the coast, the community feel authentic

### The Brand Voice Is NOT:
- Corporate or stiff
- Overly casual or slangy
- Pushy or salesy
- Vague or full of filler words

## Copy Rules

1. **Lead with trust signals** — "Licensed & Insured" and **Florida Mover Reg. #IM4125** appear above the fold on every page
2. **Use "you" and "your"** — customer-first language, not company-first
3. **Be specific about geography** — mention county and city names; it builds local credibility
4. **Avoid moving clichés** — do not write "we treat your belongings like our own" (overused). Find fresh ways to say it.
5. **Never fabricate numbers** — do not invent review counts, years in business, move counts, or awards. Use only verified facts (established 2025, 3-truck fleet, 24/7 availability).
6. **Primary CTA is quote-first** — "Get a Free Quote" (coral button) is the primary above-the-fold action sitewide; phone is secondary
7. **CTA copy is action-oriented** — "Get a Free Quote", "Call Us Now"
8. **Keep paragraphs short** — max 3 sentences. Panhandle customers are reading on mobile.
9. **No exclamation points** — calm, confident tone. Periods only.
10. **Testimonials are gated** — never render testimonials while `FLAGS.SHOW_TESTIMONIALS` is `false`; wait for verified Google/Facebook reviews
